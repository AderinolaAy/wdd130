White Water Rafting

At White Water Rafting, we believe the best adventures are shared. From scenic floats to splashing fun,
our family friendly trips are designed for all ages and skill levels. As a company that values family time,
we prioritize your safety with certified guides and premium equipment so you can focus on the smiles.
Immerse yourself in the raw power and tranquility of the river. White Water Rafting offers more than just a ride;
we offer a deep connection to the outdoors. As proud advocates, we combine eco-friendly practices with expert storytelling
to showcase the unique history and wildlife of our local waterways.

History

The White Water Rafting was founded in 1943 to provide rafting services, the company embark on a mandate that
decided that corporate world couldn't compete with the call of the canyon. With nothing but a beat-up truck and a few surplus
military rafts, White Water Rafting was born. We spent our first seasons camping on the riverbanks and learning every eddy and
rock by heart. Today, we've traded the old gear for state of the art Inflatable Technology, but that same raw love for the water still
drives every trip we lead. We've seen the river change through seasons and decades, but our mission remains the sames: to share the
Culture and Natural Resources of our home with explorers like you.
